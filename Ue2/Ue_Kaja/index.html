The Country of Others

Autor: Leila Slimani

The French-Moroccan writer Leila Slimani's third novel The Country of Others is the first book in Slimani's trilogy. A gripping tale set during World War II. Its protagonist Mathilde is a young Frenchwoman who leaves her hometown for Amine, a Moroccan who fought alongside the French. Inspired by Slimani's family history, the novel sustains an unflinching gaze at the instances of power dynamics, gender, and sexuality in a complicated social setting.

"In the Country of Others" presents a vivid tableau of life during a critical time in history.

Autorenbiografie

Leila Slimani: Geboren 1981 in Rabat, Marokko. Sie ist eine französisch-marokkanische Schriftstellerin und wurde bekannt durch ihre preisgekrönten Romane über Macht, Identität und soziale Konflikte.
