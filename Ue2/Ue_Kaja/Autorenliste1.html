Leila Slimani

Leila Slimani is a critically acclaimed French-Moroccan author and journalist. She gained international recognition for her novel "Lullaby" (Chanson douce), which won the prestigious Prix Goncourt in 2016. Her writing often explores themes such as power, gender, identity, post-colonial society, and the immigrant experience. Slimani's work is known for its bold, honest storytelling and psychological depth.
