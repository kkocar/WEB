About Leila Slimani

Leila Slimani is a French-Moroccan author and journalist. She gained international acclaim with her novel “Lullaby” (also known as “Chanson douce”), which won the prestigious Prix Goncourt in 2016.

Her work often explores complex social and personal issues with deep psychological insight.

"I write to uncover hidden truths and give voice to the silenced." - Leila Slimani

Born in 1981 in Rabat, Morocco, she studied political science and literature before becoming a full-time writer.

"I write to tell the truth — not to please." - Leila Slimani
